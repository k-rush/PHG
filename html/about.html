The Company

The ownership of PHG Solutions has been in the security and low voltage industry for over twenty five years. We fulfill the installation needs of several large contracting firms and private residence. Most of the projects we have are multi segment type Ancillary Low Voltage projects. We are looking for a few installers that are well versed in Installation that can follow blueprint diagrams and think on their own. PGH Solutions is a group of companies that have formed as a result specific needs in the industry. PGH Solutions is Paxton Security, Hi-Tek Solutions, and Greenwatch. Our company is split into four different segments and all employees will be cross trained to cover on large projects. Fire Alarm- Parts and Smarts Systems, as well as full turnkey, DAS, Mass Notification Systems.Security- Access Control, Burg, CCTV, Entry Systems, A/V- Residential and Commercial Telecom/Data- Commercial and Industrial.
