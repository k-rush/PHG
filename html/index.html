Careers

PHG Solutions is looking for dedicated and reliable candidates who desire a productive work environment and the ability to learn and grow from an experienced team.

Low Voltage Electrician

Reliable at all times Strong trouble shooting skills. Finding ground faults and using voltage meters. Need to be organized with parts and inventory. Resourceful. When working on a panel that is unfamiliar; needs to be able to work with tech support. Must be able to communicate directly with a monitoring company for zone and panel information.

Responsibilities
  • Arrive onsite at scheduled start date, communicate with your project Manager and customer.
  • Pull cabling for all listed devices in the scope of work-Install devices and terminate them-Program panel to communicate with a central station and test all zones installed
  • Train customer on the system
Qualifications
  • Must be Licensed or Certified in one or more of the following: EL-06, EL-02, EL-01,FA-1,. NICET 1-2
  • 2 year apprentices are welcome to apply
